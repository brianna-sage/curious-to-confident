We cannot teach what we have not yet examined. Before you continue these conversations with your child, take a moment to reflect honestly on your own relationship with sexuality. Your openness and healing can empower your child more than any fact or answer ever could.

Parental Self-Reflection Questions:

  • 1. What biases or judgments do you hold about sexuality?
  • 2. Have you felt safe in your own sexual exploration?
  • 3. Are you modeling a sex-positive, shame-free perspective for your teen?
  • 4. How have you contributed to the sexualization of others through thoughts or actions?
